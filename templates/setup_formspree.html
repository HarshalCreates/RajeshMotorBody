Formspree Setup for Email Notifications

This page helps you set up email notifications for your contact forms. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to formspree.io and sign up for a free account
  2. After signing in, click "New Form" and give it a name (e.g., "Rajesh Motor Body Contact")
  3. Use this email address for notifications: {{ admin_email }}
  4. After creating the form, you'll get a form ID (looks like "xrgvwpao")
  5. Update your app.py file and replace the FORMSPREE_ID value with your new form ID

Important: The first time someone submits your form, you'll receive a confirmation email from Formspree. You MUST confirm this email to start receiving notifications.
